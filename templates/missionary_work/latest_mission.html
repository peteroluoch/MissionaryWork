Mission Overview

In July 2024, Pastor Prince Obasi-Ike led a team to Kapoeta, South Sudan for a transformative mission trip. The mission focused on spreading the gospel, church building, and community support in this region that has faced significant challenges.

Despite challenging conditions, including heavy rain and difficult terrain, the team persevered in preaching the gospel across several locations. The mission was a tremendous success, with 1,138 souls receiving salvation through various outreach activities.

"Not even the heavy rain could stop the preaching of the gospel in Kapoeta. God's power was manifested, and many souls were saved."
— Pastor Prince Obasi-Ike

The team also began construction on a church building in Nanimtoit, Kapoeta North, which will serve as a permanent place of worship for the local community. Additionally, essential resources, clean water, and medical assistance were provided to meet the immediate needs of the people.

Mission Objectives

  • Conduct gospel outreach events in multiple locations across Kapoeta
  • Begin construction of a church building in Nanimtoit
  • Provide essential resources and support to the local community
  • Train local leaders for continued ministry after the mission
