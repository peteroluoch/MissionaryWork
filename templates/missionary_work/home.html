TESTIMONIALS

Transformed Lives

Hear from those whose lives have been forever changed through Pastor Prince's missionary work across Africa.

JM

John Mwangi

Nairobi, Kenya

"Pastor Prince's ministry has completely transformed my life. Through his teachings at RCCG Solution Centre, I found purpose and direction. His dedication to spreading the gospel is truly inspiring. Before I met Pastor Prince, I was lost and without hope. Now I am serving in the church and helping others find their way to Christ."

SA

Sarah Auma

Turkana, Kenya

"The church building project in our community has given us hope and a place to worship. Pastor Prince's mentorship program has also provided scholarships for our children's education. We are forever grateful. Our community in Turkana has been transformed through his ministry, and we now have access to clean water through the borehole drilling project he initiated."

DK

David Kioko

Kapoeta, South Sudan

"During the recent mission in South Sudan, I gave my life to Christ. Despite the heavy rain, Pastor Prince continued to preach the gospel. Now I am studying the Bible and growing in faith with the church community. The medical assistance provided by the mission team also helped my family when we were sick. I am now helping to build our new church in Nanimtoit."
