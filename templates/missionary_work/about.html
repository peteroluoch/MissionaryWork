Life & Ministry

Pastor Prince Okechukwu Obasi-Ike is a distinguished leader, dynamic preacher of the gospel, and an internationally recognized missionary. With a multifaceted career spanning over three decades, he has made significant strides in spreading the gospel and transforming lives across multiple countries.

Pioneer Missionary

In 1995, Pastor Prince began his journey as a pioneer missionary for the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) in Eastern Africa. His ordination as a pastor in 1997 marked the beginning of his impactful leadership at RCCG and globally.

Under Pastor Prince's stewardship, RCCG has become a beacon of hope and empowerment within communities across Africa. His missionary work has expanded to Kenya, South Sudan, Rwanda, Burundi, and Malaysia, establishing churches and providing community support.

"

My life's mission is to bring the transformative power of the gospel to every corner of Africa, empowering communities and raising leaders who will continue the work of Christ.

— Pastor Prince Obasi-Ike
"

Family Life

Pastor Prince is married to Pastor Esther Obasi-Ike, and together they work as a team to fulfill their purpose. They have enjoyed over 31 years of marriage and are blessed with two adult sons.

PRINCIPLES

Core Values

The guiding principles that shape Pastor Prince's ministry and missionary work across the globe.

Integrity & Justice

Upholding the highest standards of integrity in all dealings and advocating for justice for the marginalized and oppressed.

"He has shown you, O mortal, what is good. And what does the LORD require of you? To act justly and to love mercy and to walk humbly with your God." — Micah 6:8

Compassion

Showing Christ-like compassion for the underprivileged and responding to their needs with practical support and spiritual guidance.

"Be kind and compassionate to one another, forgiving each other, just as in Christ God forgave you." — Ephesians 4:32

Gospel Dedication

Unwavering commitment to spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ to the unreached and making disciples in all nations.

"Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit." — Matthew 28:19

Community Transformation

Empowering communities through holistic development initiatives that address spiritual, educational, and economic needs.

"Seek the peace and prosperity of the city to which I have carried you into exile. Pray to the LORD for it, because if it prospers, you too will prosper." — Jeremiah 29:7

Leadership Development

Identifying, mentoring, and equipping leaders who will continue the work of ministry and community transformation.

"And the things you have heard me say in the presence of many witnesses entrust to reliable people who will also be qualified to teach others." — 2 Timothy 2:2

Partnership & Unity

Building strategic partnerships with like-minded organizations and fostering unity within the body of Christ.

"How good and pleasant it is when God's people live together in unity!" — Psalm 133:1

Our Values in Action

These core values are not just words on a page—they are the principles that guide every aspect of Pastor Prince's ministry. From his missionary work in remote villages to church services in urban centers, these values shape how he interacts with people, makes decisions, and fulfills his calling to spread the gospel of Jesus Christ.
