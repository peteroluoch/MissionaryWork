COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Pastor Prince's ministry and missionary work across Africa and beyond.

How can I support Pastor Prince's missionary work?

You can support our missionary work through prayers, financial contributions, or by volunteering your time and skills. We welcome support in various forms:

  • Financial donations to fund mission trips and church building projects
  • Prayer support for ongoing and upcoming missions
  • Volunteering skills such as medical expertise, construction, teaching, etc.
  • Donating supplies for mission trips (Bibles, medical supplies, etc.)
Contact us for more information

Can I join a mission trip?

Yes, we welcome individuals who feel called to join our mission trips. Pastor Prince organizes several mission trips throughout the year to various countries in Africa.

To join a mission trip:

  • Fill out our mission trip application form
  • Participate in pre-mission training sessions
  • Raise the required support for your trip
  • Obtain necessary travel documents and vaccinations
Email us about upcoming mission trips

How are donations used?

Donations are used to support our missionary activities, including gospel outreach, church building, community support, and providing resources to those in need.

Your donations help fund:

  • Gospel outreach events in remote areas
  • Construction of churches in underserved communities
  • Clean water projects and humanitarian aid
  • Training and support for local pastors and leaders
View our financial transparency report

Can Pastor Prince speak at our event?

Pastor Prince is available for speaking engagements, subject to his schedule. He has spoken at numerous events across Africa, Europe, and North America.

When inviting Pastor Prince, please provide the following information:

  • Event name, date, and location
  • Expected audience size and demographic
  • Speaking topic or theme
  • Duration of speaking engagement
Email us about speaking engagements
